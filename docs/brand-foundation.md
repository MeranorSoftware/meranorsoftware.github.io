# Meranor Brand Foundation

## Locked Identity

- Company name: `Meranor`
- Tagline: `Build What Matters.`
- Core statement: `We create thoughtful software that helps people observe, create, and understand more.`
- Mission: `We believe meaningful technology should empower curiosity, not distract from it.`

## Brand Principles

1. Built with Intention
   We start with purpose and act with clarity.
2. Driven by Intelligence
   We use technology to solve meaningful problems.
3. Designed to Endure
   We build with quality, craftsmanship, and long-term thinking.
4. Focused on Impact
   We create solutions that help, inspire, and empower people to do more.

## Product Boundaries

- Meranor is a parent software, AI, and technology company.
- Meranor is not a game studio.
- Little Black Goat Games projects must not be presented as Meranor products.

## Product Family Status

- `FieldKeeper`: first flagship, still in development
- `Astra AI`: in development
- `Geomancer`: in development
- `PatchCube`: early concept or development
- `CountLens`: internal or in-development software

## Visual Direction

- Palette:
  - near-black and charcoal backgrounds
  - stone and warm-white typography
  - muted graphite surfaces
  - warm brass accent
  - restrained deep cobalt only when needed
- Tone:
  - premium
  - modern
  - intelligent
  - cinematic
  - calm
  - intentional

## Avoid

- bright AI blue as the dominant accent
- neon gradients
- cyberpunk or crypto aesthetics
- stock imagery from the web
- fake launch claims
- fake testimonials
- fake metrics
- fake social, legal, or contact details
