Building software like naturalists build field guides

Preview sample content about observation-first product design and FieldKeeper's calm journal sensibility.

Preview sample content June 18, 2026

Why this piece exists

This entry sets the voice and structure for future workshop writing while the permanent publishing workflow is still being assembled.

Naturalists do not start with noise. They start by looking carefully, recording faithfully, and returning often enough to notice patterns.

That same discipline matters in software. We want FieldKeeper to feel less like a dashboard and more like a trusted field guide: calm in hand, clear in structure, and useful long after the first interaction.

The product questions are simple on purpose

  • What did you notice?
  • Where did you notice it?
  • What changed over time?
  • What story does the pattern tell?

That is the kind of software we want Meranor to make.
